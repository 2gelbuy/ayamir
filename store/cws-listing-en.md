## Detailed Description
AyaMir — Protect your time, build your legacy.

A local-first productivity extension that combines smart task management, deep work sessions, and site blocking into one powerful tool. No accounts. No cloud. No tracking. Everything stays on your device.

SMART TASK MANAGEMENT
• Priority-based task queue with smart sorting
• Inline editing, snooze, and due dates
• Quick task from any page with Ctrl+Shift+K
• Right-click any page to save as a task

DEEP WORK MODE
• Pomodoro timer (15 / 25 / 45 / 60 / 90 min)
• Circular progress ring with real-time countdown
• Hard Lock Mode — can't cancel once started
• Automatic site blocking during focus sessions

SITE BLOCKING
• One-click category presets: Social, News, Entertainment, Shopping
• Custom domain blocking
• Scheduled blocking (auto-block during work hours)
• Typing challenge to bypass — makes you think twice

GAMIFICATION
• Earn XP for completing tasks and focus sessions
• 10 levels from Beginner to Transcendent
• Achievements: Early Bird, Night Owl, Speed Demon, and more
• Daily streaks to keep you motivated

100% PRIVATE
• All data stored locally in your browser
• Zero network requests — works completely offline
• No accounts, no servers, no analytics
• Open source (MIT License)

KEYBOARD SHORTCUTS
• N or / — New task
• D — Deep Work mode
• V — View stats
• S — Settings
• ? — All shortcuts

Available in: English, Russian, Spanish, Chinese, Hindi

★ Free forever. No ads. No tracking. Just focus.
