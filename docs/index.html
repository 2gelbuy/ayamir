Frequently Asked Questions

Is AyaMir really free?

Yes. All core features are available at no cost. No ads, no tracking. The source code is open under the MIT License.

Does AyaMir collect my data?

No. AyaMir makes zero network requests. All your tasks, settings, and statistics are stored locally in your browser. Nothing ever leaves your device.

How is AyaMir different from StayFocusd or Cold Turkey?

AyaMir combines four tools in one: task manager, Pomodoro timer, website blocker, and gamification. StayFocusd and Cold Turkey are primarily site blockers without task management or XP systems. AyaMir is also completely free and open source.

Can I use AyaMir as a Pomodoro timer?

Yes. AyaMir includes a full Pomodoro timer with presets (15, 25, 45, 60, 90 minutes), customizable break timer, circular progress ring, and a Hard Lock Mode that prevents you from canceling a session.

Does AyaMir work offline?

Yes. AyaMir makes zero network requests and works completely offline. All features work without an internet connection.

What languages does AyaMir support?

AyaMir is available in English, Russian, Spanish, Chinese (Simplified), and Hindi. The interface automatically matches your browser language.

Can I export my data?

Yes. AyaMir includes full data export and import in JSON format. You can back up all your tasks, settings, focus sessions, and achievements at any time.
